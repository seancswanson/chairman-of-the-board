Start your corporate career as a Mailroom Clerk and try to climb the ranks as you encounter scenarios and events that could skyrocket you to your Golden Throne as Chairman of the Board or be derailed and spend eternity in that god-forsaken letter hell!

Earn confidence points for proper reactions to each week's scenario and if your manager especially approves of your decision you may earn brownie points! Every 5 weeks you have the chance to earn a promotion and can use confidence to increase your chances or if you have enough brownie points you may be given special priveledges! If you don't have enough points then you may continue the corporate grind and earn more points.
